FAQ

How do I book a parking slot?

Simply log in to your ParkSmart account, select your desired slot, and proceed to make the booking and payment.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking and receive a refund as per our cancellation policy.

How does the QR code work?

The QR code is generated upon successful booking and serves as your digital parking pass. Simply scan it at the parking entrance for seamless entry.

What if I can't find my booked slot?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you in locating your booked slot and ensuring a smooth parking experience.

How can owners monitor parking activities?

Owners and admins have access to a real-time dashboard that provides insights into parking bookings, payments, and user activities.
